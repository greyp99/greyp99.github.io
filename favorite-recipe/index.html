Bacon and Egg Sandwich

A delicous breakfast sandwich with an omelet base and bacon. Tastes really good with a cream cheese of your choice!

Ingredients

  • Ingredients
    • 3 eggs
    • 1/2 tbsp salt
    • 1/4 tbsp pepper
    • 1/2 tbsp butter
    • 1/2 tbsp butter spread
    • 1/4 cup milk
    • 2 slices bread of your choice
    • cream cheese or any other sauce of your choice
